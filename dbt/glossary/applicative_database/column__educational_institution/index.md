**educational_institution_id**: Id of the educational institution.

**educational_scholar_year**: scholar year for which the grant was received

**educational_redactor_id**: Id of the educational redactor.

**ministry**: The ministry to which the educational institution belongs. Values can be : AGRICULTURE; AGRICULTURE; EDUCATION_NATIONALE; MER.

**current_deposit_amount**: The current amount of deposit for the educational institution.

**current_deposit_creation_date**: The date when the current deposit was created for the educational institution.

**last_category_booked**: The last category booked by the educational institution.

**total_current_calendar_year_deposit_amount**: Total deposit of the current calendar year.

**total_current_scholar_year_deposit_amount**: Total deposit of the current scholar year.

**total_theoretic_amount_spent**: Total theoric amount spent (= non cancelled pre-reservations) by the school since the first deposit.

**total_current_scholar_year_theoretic_amount_spent**: Total theoric amount spent (= non cancelled pre-reservations) by the school during the current scholar_year.

**total_real_amount_spent**: Total real amount spent (= used and reimbursed reservations only) by the school since the first deposit.

**total_current_scholar_year_real_amount_spent**: Total real amount spent (= used and reimbursed reservations only) by the school during the current scholar_year.

**ratio_current_scholar_year_credit_utilization**: Total real amount spent (= used and reimbursed reservations only) by the school during the current scholar_year divided by the deposit of the current scholar year.
