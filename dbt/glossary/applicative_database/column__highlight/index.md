**highlight_request_id**: Unique identifier of the highlight request.

**highlight_id**: Unique identifier of the highlight itself ("temps de valorisation thématique").

**highlight_image_id**: Identifier of the visual or image used to represent this highlight.

**highlight_name**: Label of the highlight.

**highlight_description**: Detailed and complete description of the content or purpose of the highlight.

**highlight_beginning_date**: Start date when the highlight is actively displayed to users within the app.

**highlight_ending_date**: End date when the highlight is actively displayed to users within the app.

**highlight_availability_beginning_date**: Start date when cultural partners can apply or submit their offers for the highlight selection.

**highlight_availability_ending_date**: End date when cultural partners can apply or submit their offers for the highlight selection.

**highlight_communication_date**: Date on which the cultural partner is formally notified whether their submission for the highlight has been selected for display.
