**pro_label**: Name of the offer subcategory as it appears on the platform dedicated to cultural partners.

**app_label**: Name of the offer subcategory as it appears on the platform dedicated to beneficiaries and individual users.

**can_be_educational**: Boolean field, indicates if this subcategory can be used to create collective offers.

**reimbursement_rule**: Indicates the reimbursement rule applied to the bookings of this subcategory. Different types of rules : STANDARD (standard rate applies), BOOK (custom rate for books) or NOT_REIMBURSED (no reimbursement towards the cultural partner for bookings in this subcategory).

**can_expire**: Boolean field, indicates whether bookings in this subcategory can expire.

Boolean field that indicates whether a reservation in this category will impact the portion of the grant that the beneficiary can dedicate to physical reservations.

Boolean field that indicates whether a reservation in this category will impact the portion of the grant that the beneficiary can dedicate to digital reservations.

**can_be_duo**: Boolean field, indicates whether bookings in this subcategory can be made in duo (two tickets purchased).
