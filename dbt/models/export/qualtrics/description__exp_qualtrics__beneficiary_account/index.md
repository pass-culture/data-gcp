Monthly random sample of pass Culture beneficiary users exported to Qualtrics for NPS reputation indicator surveys.

- 10,000 users aged 15-17 (GRANT_15_17) and 10,000 aged 18+ (GRANT_18), randomly selected each month
- Excludes users exported in the previous month
- Excludes users who have opted out of Qualtrics emails
- Excludes users who have already answered a survey

Destination: `raw_{env}.qualtrics_exported_beneficiary_account` (monthly partition, audit) Qualtrics mailing list: "[Import] Indicateur de réputation Jeunes"
