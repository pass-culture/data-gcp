Monthly random sample of pass Culture cultural venues exported to Qualtrics for NPS reputation indicator surveys.

- 4,000 venues randomly selected each month
- Excludes venues exported in the previous 3 months
- Excludes venues whose booking email has opted out of Qualtrics emails
- Excludes venues whose manager has already answered a survey

Destination: `raw_{env}.qualtrics_exported_venue_account` (monthly partition, audit) Qualtrics mailing list: "[Import] Indicateur de réputation Acteur Culturel"
