The `metrics_beneficiary__expired_cohorts` model provides aggregated key indicators related to pass Culture beneficary expired cohorts. It is designed to be the central model providing expired cohorts indicators for export, monitoring and gouvernance. The credit allocated at 18 years old remains valid and available until the eve of the beneficiary's 21st birthday. The remaining credit strictly expires.

## Table Description
